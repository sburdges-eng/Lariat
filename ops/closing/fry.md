# Closing — Fry

> Source: `Closing checklist_ Positions_.docx`

---

## During slowdown

- [ ] Restock / top-fill station
- [ ] Flip station
- [ ] Refill / replace sauce bottles

## Shutdown

- [ ] Turn off fryers (left two + right)
- [ ] **Clean / filter fryer oil** — see schedule below
- [ ] Sanitize surfaces
- [ ] Wrap lowboy
- [ ] Check GFI outlets

## Before clock-out

- [ ] LINE CHECK — final closing line check
- [ ] Clock out

---

## Fryer oil schedule

Oil filtration is **every close**. Full boil-out is **Sunday** — see
[../cleaning/weekly.md](../cleaning/weekly.md).
