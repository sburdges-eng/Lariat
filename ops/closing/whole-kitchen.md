# Closing — Whole Kitchen (House-Close)

The master sequence. **Do not** clock out until step 10.

> Source: `Closing procedures.docx`

---

1. [ ] Shut off steam well, grill, fryer, and oven
2. [ ] Restock reach-in
3. [ ] Wipe down stainless surfaces, cutting boards
4. [ ] Wash and put away dishes. Drain dishwasher
5. [ ] Sweep and mop floor. Empty mop bucket
6. [ ] Shut off gas line to wok station
7. [ ] Check all closing procedures have been done (walk every station)
8. [ ] Shut off hood vent
9. [ ] Double-check all closing procedures have been done
10. [ ] Clock out
11. [ ] Log close-of-day **walk-in + freezer temps** → [../temps/README.md](../temps/README.md)

---
