# Opening — Whole Kitchen

Master sequence. The first cook in runs this, then each station runs their own
station-specific opening.

**Target: kitchen open at 11:00 AM (lunch/dinner) or per BEO for brunch.**

---

## 1. Arrival (T-3 hours)

- Clock in
- Wash hands
- Flip main lights
- Unlock walk-in and check temps **before** pulling anything (see [../temps/README.md](../temps/README.md))
- Log walk-in cooler + walk-in freezer temps on [today's temp log](../temps/README.md)
- Turn on hood vent

## 2. Fire up the line (T-3 to T-2:30)

- Turn on ovens, grill, salamander, steam wells, fryers (set per station)
- Turn on Sally (brunch-only)
- Turn on heat lamps (salad + expo)
- Fill sani buckets for every station (change every 2 hours during service, or when visibly dirty)

## 3. Station-by-station sequence

Run these in parallel — they each take ~60 min:

- [Grill / Saute →](grill.md)
- [Salad (Garde) →](salad.md)
- [Fry →](fry.md)
- Expo — set up steam table, cutting board + hotel pan for sauces/toppings, refill spoon bucket, line baskets, restock chips, input counts in Toast.

## 4. T-30: line check

Every station runs their line check on the iPad at T-30. KM (or senior cook on
duty) reviews all four line-check sign-offs before doors open.

→ [line-checks/README.md](../line-checks/README.md)

## 5. T-0: open

- Confirm all line checks green on `/today` page
- Confirm no active 86s blocking menu items on the board
- Unlock kitchen pass

---

## Brunch variation

Brunch opens earlier (09:00 AM clock-in, 11:00 AM doors). Key deltas from
weekday lunch:

- Set up waffle iron, egg station, tostadas
- Warm up queso, quesa birria + consume, green chile
- Cook off bacon for bar + expo
- Cold line extras: yuzu kosho, 1/9 cherry tomatoes, shallow 1/6 mixed greens
  tossed in lemon thyme, 1/9 mashed avocado, 1 bottle Cali pepper oil, dulce de
  leche + powdered sugar
- Line check at 10:30
- Breakfast potatoes to grill at 10:45
