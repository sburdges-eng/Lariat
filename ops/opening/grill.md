# Opening — Grill / Saute (Hot Line)

Target: station ready for line check at **T-30** (3:30 PM for a 4:00 opening /
10:30 AM for an 11:00 brunch open). Clock-in at T-3 (2:00 PM).

> Source: `GRILL SET-UP.xlsx` + `Setups.docx` → "Hot line setup and procedure"

---

## 2:00 — arrive + unwrap

- [ ] Clock in, wash hands
- [ ] Turn on ovens, grill, salamander
- [ ] Gather sani bucket, tools, towels
- [ ] Unwrap all drawers and lowboy proteins
- [ ] Log **grill lowboy temp** → see [../temps/README.md](../temps/README.md)

## 2:15–2:30 — sauces on

In sauce pots, heat:

| Sauce          | Pan size           |
| -------------- | ------------------ |
| Green chile    | 1/2 + 1/6          |
| Queso          | 1/2 + 1/6          |
| Chicken jus    | 1/9                |
| Birria         | 1/3                |
| Consume        | 1/3                |

- [ ] Add water to 1/6 pan and chafe 1qt deli of bacon jam on right-hand side of flat top
- [ ] Fill 1/9 metal insert with salt and pepper
- [ ] Top off squeeze bottle of clarified butter and temper **2qt+** for back up
- [ ] Top off oil and wine

## 2:30–2:45 — bread + proteins

- [ ] Stock buns, sourdough, and summer toast boule
- [ ] Check protein back ups:
  - [ ] 2/3 pan burgers
  - [ ] 1/3 pan jeffe
  - [ ] 1/3 pan cooked bacon
  - If below par → pull / portion / cook back ups
- [ ] Stock **6 orders ± chicken + cod**
- [ ] In a 1/3 pan with ice stock **5 orders ± trout and pork chop**

## 2:45–3:00 — finish + mid-week par

- [ ] Mid-week par on lettuce (**3/3**) and tomatoes (**2/6**)
- [ ] 1 backup of all other items in the roll top (noodles, burger prep,
      1qt shredded cheese below)
- [ ] Temper honey butter and herb butter
- [ ] Ensure 4.5" white corn tortillas stocked + backed up
- [ ] Stock and back up white flour tortillas
- [ ] Stock ramekins
- [ ] Pull plates forward and split stacks if needed

## 3:30 — LINE CHECK

→ [../line-checks/grill-saute.md](../line-checks/grill-saute.md)

Sign off on iPad before service.
