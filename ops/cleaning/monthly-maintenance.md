# Monthly Cleaning & Maintenance

Scheduled monthly — one Sunday a month after close, or on a slow Monday AM.

> Source: `Monthly Cleaning_Maintenance.docx`

---

## Checklist

- [ ] Sweep, degrease, and scrub floors (walk-in, freezer, shed, etc.)
- [ ] Wipe down / polish stainless (hoods, tables, reach-ins, lowboys, ice machine, etc.)
- [ ] Clean containers (qt cups, spice containers, etc. — **consolidate**)
- [ ] Clean storage lexans / hotel pans
- [ ] Re-organize / consolidate storage lexans / hotel pans
- [ ] Wipe down coils / cords / Toast monitors
- [ ] Organize event space (loft above lockers / walk-in)

---

## Equipment maintenance (tie to `/equipment`)

Each piece of equipment has its own maintenance cadence — the app tracks the
last time it was serviced:

- **Hoods** — filter soak Sunday weekly; deep clean monthly above
- **Fryer oil** — filter daily, full boil-out Sunday
- **Ice machine** — sanitize monthly
- **Walk-in + freezer evaporator coils** — wipe monthly; quarterly by vendor
- **Toast screens + printers** — wipe monthly

→ See `/equipment` for the live log and vendor contacts.

## Schedule

Monthly jobs are scheduled via the launchd reminders in
[../launchd/](../launchd/) and mirrored in `/admin/cleaning-schedule` → Monthly.

Sign-off: `/admin/cleaning-schedule` → Monthly → sign off (initial + date).
