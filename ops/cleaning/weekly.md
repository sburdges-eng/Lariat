# Weekly Cleaning — Wed → Sun

A rolling deep-clean. Every day of the back half of the week has a job. The
Monday/Tuesday gap is intentional — that's when we're running weekly prep
(see [../prep/weekly-prep-wednesday.md](../prep/weekly-prep-wednesday.md)).

> Source: `Weekly Cleaning.docx`

---

## Wednesday

- [ ] Scrub underneath equipment
- [ ] Wipe down walls
- [ ] Clean out walk-ins

## Thursday

- [ ] Organize shed
- [ ] Wipe down tools (scales, Robot Coupe, etc.)
- [ ] Wipe down prep table bottoms

## Friday

- [ ] Wipe down screens
- [ ] Wipe down cords
- [ ] Wipe down heat lamps

## Saturday

- [ ] Organize spices
- [ ] Organize shelves (ceramics, mixing bowls, hotel pans, etc.)
- [ ] Clean out reach-ins

## Sunday

- [ ] Soak cast iron (range stars, grill grates)
- [ ] Soak hoods
- [ ] Clean out drip pans
- [ ] **Boil out fryers**
