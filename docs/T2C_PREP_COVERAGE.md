## Density caveat — don't pad the seed with garbage

The 111 unhit ingredients on densities are dominated by **solids** (garlic, sugar, cilantro, butter, dill, jalapenos, parsley, brown sugar, capers, chicken stock, coarse salt, coriander, green chile, jalapeno, lemons, red pepper, rosemary, thyme, achiote paste, anchovy, arbol chiles, …). These are measured by weight, not volume — they should NOT have density entries.

The 49.7% line-weighted figure is the correct ceiling for a clean density seed. Pushing it above 50% by adding fake `g_per_ml` values for solids would corrupt the table. T2c spec gates on `yield_pct` coverage, so this is a non-issue — the density coverage number is informational only.
