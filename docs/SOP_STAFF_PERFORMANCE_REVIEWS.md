# SOP: Staff Performance Reviews

**Scope:** Management / Labor Compliance
**Stakeholders:** General Manager (GM), Kitchen Manager (KM)
**Target:** Back-of-house staff (Cooks, Prep, Dish)

## 1. Objective
To maintain a high standard of service and technical execution through regular, structured feedback. Performance reviews in Lariat are designed to be lightweight, manager-only logs that track three core kitchen metrics: **On Time (Punctuality)**, **Technique**, and **Speed**.

## 2. Review Cadence
- **Weekly Check-ins:** Managers are encouraged to log at least one review per cook every two weeks.
- **Alerts:** If no reviews are logged for the current shift date, the **Command Center** will show an amber alert.

## 3. Scoring Rubric (1–5)

Reviews are classified based on the average of the three scores.

| Average Score | Classification | Status (UI) | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **4.5 – 5.0** | Exceptional | **Green** | Recognize at preshift; consider for "Gold Star". |
| **4.0 – 4.4** | Great | **Green** | Standard high performance. |
| **3.0 – 3.9** | Good | **Amber** | Solid performance; identified areas for growth. |
| **2.5 – 2.9** | Solid | **Amber** | Meeting minimum requirements. |
| **< 2.5** | Needs Improvement | **Red** | Requires immediate supervisor intervention or retraining. |

### Metric Definitions:
- **On Time:** Arrival for shift, return from breaks, and readiness for station setup.
- **Technique:** Knife skills, adherence to recipes, sanitation habits, and station cleanliness.
- **Speed:** Ticket times, prep efficiency, and ability to handle rushes without compromising quality.

## 4. Documentation Rules
- **Notes:** Use the "Notes" field for specific observations (e.g., "Handled the Friday rush perfectly," or "Needs to sharpen knife skills on mirepoix").
- **Audit:** All reviews are logged to the **Management Audit Log**. Modifications or removals are tracked for HR transparency.
- **Privacy:** Performance reviews are **Manager-Only**. They do not appear on staff-facing boards (like the Gold Star board).

## 5. System Integration
- **Command Center:** Summary counts and "Logged Today" lists.
- **Labor Hub:** Compliance tracking and status colors.
- **Kitchen Assistant:** Grounded AI context for manager queries (e.g., "How has Alice performed lately?").
- **Exports:** Daily CSV/XLSX reports for payroll and regional records.
