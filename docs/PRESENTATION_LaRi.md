# Pitch Deck Outline: The LaRi Cockpit

## Slide 1: The Modern Restaurant Software Trap
**Title:** The Fragmentation of Restaurant Operations
*   **The Status Quo:** Restaurants are forced to piece together a mosaic of expensive, disconnected SaaS subscriptions.
*   **The Costs (2026 Estimates):**
    *   **Restaurant365 (R365):** $249 to $499+/month per location (Core to Essential tiers).
    *   **Toast POS:** $69 to $400+/month per location (depending on modules).
    *   **7shifts (Labor):** $35 to $150+/month per location + payroll add-ons.
    *   **Prism.fm (Events/BEO):** $150 to $300+/month per location.
    *   **Total:** Easily exceeding $800–$1,300+ per month per location in pure software fees, not including payment processing.
*   **The Real Cost:** Data is siloed. Your sales data (Toast), labor data (7shifts), and food costs (Sysco/Shamrock) don't talk to each other without hours of manual spreadsheet work or paying for expensive monolithic platforms like R365.

## Slide 2: Introducing LaRi
**Title:** LaRi: The Unified Back-of-House Cockpit
*   **What it is:** A local-first, deterministic software suite that acts as the central nervous system for your restaurant.
*   **How it works:** It automatically ingests and normalizes data from your existing tools (Toast, 7shifts, Prism, Sysco, Shamrock) into a single, offline-capable database.
*   **The Result:** Instant visibility into margins, inventory, and operations without replacing your core vendors.

## Slide 3: Why Invest in LaRi? (Core Benefits)
**Title:** Control Your Data, Maximize Your Margins
1.  **Automated Menu Engineering & Costing:**
    *   LaRi cross-references live Toast sales with dynamic Sysco/Shamrock vendor pricing.
    *   *Benefit:* Instantly identify margin shrinkage, price shocks, and perform "what-if" costing without manual data entry.
2.  **Local-First & Offline Reliability:**
    *   Runs locally on a Mac with iPads. Zero dependency on cloud uptime. If the internet goes down during a Friday dinner rush, LaRi keeps running.
3. **Zero-Cost, Privacy-First AI:**
    *   Features a grounded "Kitchen Assistant" powered by local AI (**DeepSeek R1 14B**).
    *   *Benefit:* Answers recipe, inventory, and prep questions instantly based on *your* actual data with state-of-the-art reasoning capabilities. No monthly OpenAI API fees, and your proprietary recipes never leave the building.

4.  **Bulletproof Compliance (HACCP & Labor):**
    *   Built-in, fully audited workflows for temperature logs, cooling, sick workers, and labor breaks (CO COMPS #39, HFWA).
    *   *Benefit:* Replaces paper logs with an immutable, auditor-ready digital trail. 

## Slide 4: The Financial ROI
**Title:** Recouping the Investment
*   **Eliminate "Ghost" SaaS Modules:** By centralizing features, you can downgrade premium tiers of other software (e.g., dropping expensive inventory/recipe add-ons from Toast or third parties).
*   **Prevent Margin Leakage:** Real-time theoretical vs. actual cost variance catches shrink and price hikes before the end-of-month P&L.
*   **Operational Efficiency:** Save 10+ hours a week for Kitchen Managers by automating the ingestion of invoices, sales summaries, and BEO event mapping.
*   **Perpetual Ownership:** You own the system and the data. No surprise annual price hikes.
